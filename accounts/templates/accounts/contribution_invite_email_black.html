What is Revibe?

Revibe is an all-in-one music platform for independent artists. We believe everyone should be able to monetize their art, and our goal is to provide you with all the tools necessary to do so.

 

Through Revibe you can host music, distribute songs, sell merchandise, buy and sell beats and other creative services through the marketplace, and more!

 

If that's not enough, check out Revibe Music, our mobile streaming application that lets you stream Spotify, YouTube, and Revibe's hosted tracks all through one app. Queue songs, create playlists, and discover music from you favorite underground artists to mainstream stars alike. Sign up now!
